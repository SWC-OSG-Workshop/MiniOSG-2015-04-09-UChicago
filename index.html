General Information

The Open Science Grid (OSG) is a national scale distributed infrastructure for scientific computing. The OSG also offers training and tutorials for the scientists and researchers. As part of our training service we are offering hands on workshop on distributed high throughput computing (DHTC). Participants will be encouraged to help one another and to apply what they have learned to their own research problems. For more information, please email: balamurugan@uchicago.edu .
